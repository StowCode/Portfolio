Hello, I'm Mitch. For the last 7 years, I have been a music educator in New Jersey. I have professional experience designing competitive marching band shows, working with brands to coordinate sponsorship and fundraising, leading teams of staff and students, organizing large scale events, and facilitating relationships with experts to ensure the highest level product is being created.
