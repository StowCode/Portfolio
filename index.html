About Me

Hey, I'm Mitch.

For the last 7 years, I have been a music educator in New Jersey. I’ve learned many things in my time as an educator, but most importantly, I’ve learned that I’m happiest when I’m creating. When a friend introduced me to coding, I became enamored with the process of software design and the potential it carries. I have professional experience designing competitive marching band shows, working with brands to coordinate sponsorship and fundraising, managing projects and leading teams of staff and students, organizing large scale events, and facilitating relationships with experts to ensure the highest level product is being created.

As an educator, and even more so a music educator, problem solving and creative solutions are a way of life. I’m excited to bring my experience into the world of tech.
